The Haunted Mansion

Once upon a time

In a remote village shrouded in mist, there stood a mansion that cast a dark shadow over the land.

Locals whispered tales of its haunted corridors and the restless spirit of its former owner, a reclusive noble who met a tragic end.

Brave souls who dared to venture inside spoke of eerie whispers in the dead of night, phantom footsteps echoing through empty halls, and mysterious lights flickering in long-abandoned rooms.

But amidst the fear and superstition, there were those who believed that within the mansion's walls lay secrets waiting to be uncovered...
